## üîß Technical Questions

### Q: Does AdGuard slow down my internet?
**A:** Actually, pages load faster without ads. You may see 2-3x faster loading on ad-heavy sites.

### Q: Will it break websites?
**A:** Rarely. If it does, you can:
1. Add the site to whitelist
2. Report to filter maintainers
3. Use "Filtering log" to find and disable specific rules

### Q: Is HTTPS filtering safe?
**A:** Yes. AdGuard installs a local certificate to filter encrypted traffic. Your data stays private and never leaves your device.

### Q: Does it work with VPN?
**A:** Yes, but on Android you need to use HTTP proxy mode instead of VPN mode if running another VPN.

## üì± Platform-Specific

### Q: Why isn't AdGuard on Google Play?
**A:** Google banned ad blockers from Play Store. Download the official APK from adguard.com instead.

### Q: Does it work on iPhone?
**A:** Yes, but due to iOS limitations, it's Safari-only (system-wide blocking not possible on iOS).

### Q: Will it block YouTube ads?
**A:** On desktop: Yes. On mobile apps: Limited due to YouTube's encryption.

### Q: AdGuard vs uBlock Origin?
**A:** 
- **uBlock:** Free, browser-only, open-source
- **AdGuard:** Premium features, system-wide, dedicated support

### Q: AdGuard vs Pi-hole?
**A:**
- **Pi-hole:** Network-level, requires separate device
- **AdGuard:** Device-level, easier setup, more features
